Chicken Curry

Page Source: International Cuisine

  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • bone-in/boneless chicken
  • 3 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger and garlic paste
  • chilli powder as preferred
  • 2 large sliced tomatoes
  • garam masala
  1. mix turmeric and salt together
  2. rub it with chicken and let in marinate for 30 mins
  3. heat the oil over medium-heat
  4. Add garlic, ginger, chilli powder and some garam masala and stir all
  5. drop the marinated chicken and stir fry for 4-6 minutes, then add tomatoes
  6. Bring it to boil, then decrease the heat and simmer it for next 15-20 minutes
  7. Sprinkle with some chopped cilantro and serve it

It's a combination of spices and so many flavors that brings a different taste to it. Also, my mother used to make it for me when I was at home, so it carries a lot of memories for me as well. It brings a feeling of home to me.
